Providers Treating the Mind Body and Mental Health Patient by Patient

Medizo is a trusted name of Medical Services who is always at your side and your health is our first priority.

Meet Benedeth N Nwumeh our provider who is a dual board-certified Psychiatric mental health nurse practitioner and Family Nurse Practitioner. Benedeth Nwumeh has been a Nurse Practitioner for more than six years.

Benedeth focuses more now on mental wellness managing both adults and adolescents with anxiety, depression, ADHD, ADD, personality disorders, mood disorders, schizophrenia, bipolar, eating disorders, and more.

She has more than 30 years of clinical experience in health care both in and outside the United States. Dr. Bene as she is often called earned her Master of Science in Nursing degree from Governors States University, and a Doctor of Nursing Practice degree with a Specialty in Psychiatric and Mental health from the University of Massachusetts Global.

Dr. Bene treats her patients with integrity, compassion, and respect while observing professional standards. She is a good listener which enables her to understand her patients and appropriately address their needs. Her motto is to treat others as one will like to be treated.

Ronnels Healthcare offers GeneSight testing a genetic test that identifies how your genes may affect the success of a drug treatment in your mental health treatment. The GeneSight test may identify which medications that you may respond better to if you have depression, anxiety, ADHD, and other mental health conditions depending on your genetic make-up.

It is simple to do, a kit is mailed to your home, all you need to do is to swab the inside of your cheek according to the instructions in the package, and then send the kit back. A Comprehensive report, personalized for you about which medications may require dose adjustments, be less likely to work, or have an increased risk of side effects based on your genetic information.
