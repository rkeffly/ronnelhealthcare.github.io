Ronnels Mind Body and Mental Wellness

Ronnels cares about individual's total wellbeing brcause body syatems can not exist without the other. Wellness can only be achieved when the mind, and body mental are well aligned. Wellness is the act of practicing healthy habits on a daily basis to attain better physical and mental health outcomes so that instead of just surviving, you’re thriving.

Even the World Health Organization recognized health as “a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being”. Ronnels healthcare knows the importance of thriving instead of surviving, and will therefore help you navigate stressful life struggles through psychotherapy and medication management.

Challenges with mental health illnesses are real and can be difficult to deal with daily. At Ronnels mind, body, and mental wellness we listen to and examine your mental health in relation to your genetic predisposition, environmental, social, emotional, and spirituality and then help you navigate the challenges.


Conditions We Treat

Bipolar Disorder

Is a mood disorder that affects an individual’s concentration, energy, and activity level. These affect the patient’s ability to carry out their daily lives.

Attention-Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)

Is a neurodevelopmental mental health disorder, symptoms usually begin in childhood or early adolescence, but may not be diagnosed until adulthood.

Anxiety

Is excessive worrying and difficulty controlling a situation leading to restlessness, fatigue, difficulty concentrating irritability, muscle tension, and sleep disturbances

Schizophrenia

Is a serious mental illness that affects people’s thinking, feeling, and behavior. They may be losing reality making it difficult to relate with people and perform daily activities. With early treatment, independence, and relationships can be achieved.

Depression

Is a mood disorder with symptoms of sadness, depressed mood, irritability, anhedonia, lack of motivation feelings of hopelessness sleep disturbances. They may be low energy, appetite, and sleep disturbance. Depression can range from mild to severe and is a highly treatable disorder.

Ronnels Healthcare offers GeneSight testing a genetic test that identifies how your genes may affect the success of a drug treatment in your mental health treatment. The GeneSight test may identify which medications that you may respond better to if you have depression, anxiety, ADHD, and other mental health conditions depending on your genetic make-up.

It is simple to do, a kit is mailed to your home, all you need to do is to swab the inside of your cheek according to the instructions in the package, and then send the kit back. A Comprehensive report, personalized for you about which medications may require dose adjustments, be less likely to work, or have an increased risk of side effects based on your genetic information.
